Keep article titles and source names in English.

2. Improve readability
- Make all KPI cards equal height.
- KPI cards should contain only label, main value, and a very short subtitle.
- Remove long explanations from inside KPI cards.
- Move explanations into a collapsible expander named "대시보드 해석 가이드".
- Keep the design clean, corporate, blue and white.
- Reduce excess vertical spacing.
- Make charts easier to read.

3. Add freshness indicators
Add a top section or small info row showing:
- 최종 업데이트 시각: when RSS data was last fetched
- 최신 기사 날짜: the most recent article published date in the collected data
- 데이터 수집 기준: recent 7 days / 30 days depending on selected filter

Use India Standard Time for timestamps if possible.

4. Add date range filter
Add a sidebar filter:
- 최근 1일
- 최근 7일
- 최근 30일

Use the selected range in Google News RSS queries:
- 1 day: when:1d
- 7 days: when:7d
- 30 days: when:30d

Default should be 최근 7일.

5. Data quality section
Keep the Data Quality Check section collapsed by default.
It should show:
- Raw articles collected
- Articles after deduplication
- Other ratio
- Latest article date
- Last fetched time
- Warning if the latest article is older than 3 days:
  "최신 기사 날짜가 3일 이상 오래되었습니다. RSS 수집 상태를 확인하세요."

6. Keep the app deployable on Streamlit Community Cloud.
Make sure requirements.txt includes all dependencies.
